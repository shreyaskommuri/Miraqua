Step 01

Valves and soil sensors in the field

Miraqua's solar-powered controller connects to your existing valves and reads soil moisture directly from in-ground sensors — giving you real conditions, not estimates.

Soil sensorsExisting valves Solar poweredNo wiring

Step 02

Researched crop model meets live data

Sensor readings combine with live weather and evapotranspiration data. Researched crop modeling formulas — based on FAO and USDA agronomy — compute the exact water demand for your crop's current growth stage.

ET₀ modelCrop coefficients FAO & USDA formulasLive weather

Step 03

Farmer knowledge shapes the final plan

Farmers know their crops better than any model. Your inputs and preferences pass through an AI layer that adapts the recommendation — so the plan reflects your expertise, not just the numbers.

Farmer preferencesAI adaptation Per-zone planSkip on rain

Step 04

Automatic execution — or just ask

Miraqua executes the plan automatically through your existing valves and systems. Or use the AI assistant for real-time control: "Skip tomorrow" or "What changed this week?" — answered instantly.
