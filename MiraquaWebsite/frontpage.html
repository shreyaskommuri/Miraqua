How It Works

Miraqua calculates your farm's daily water needs by combining your inputs with powerful data sources:

  • User inputs like crop type, land area, rainfall, root depth, and soil moisture
  • Live weather and soil data pulled from trusted external APIs
  • A machine learning model trained on UC Merced agricultural and climate research
  • An algorithm that estimates water needs using both farmer inputs and real-time conditions
  • Final recommendations cross-check both methods to ensure accuracy and consistency

Future Plans

Miraqua is built to scale across regions and farm sizes. We’re designing for flexibility, efficiency, and sustainability — now and into the future.

  • Sensor compatibility: Our tool works with soil moisture sensors and flow meters for enhanced accuracy.
  • Minimal input needed: Runs with just ZIP code, land area, and crop type — no hardware required.
  • Efficiency-focused: Designed to apply the least water necessary while maintaining healthy crop growth.
  • Ideal for small and mid-sized farms that can't afford expensive irrigation systems.
  • Scalable and region-adaptable thanks to sensor data and real-time environmental inputs.
  • Easily integrates with existing infrastructure in large-scale operations for optimized automation.
